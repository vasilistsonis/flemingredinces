01 | The Building

Eight private residences, one address on the Athenian Riviera.

Designed as an exclusive collection of just eight private residences, the building redefines contemporary Riviera living through a carefully balanced architectural language of privacy, openness and refined simplicity.

Landscaped gardens, spacious terraces and private roof gardens extend the living experience into the open air, while thoughtfully composed volumes, natural materials and abundant daylight create a sense of timeless elegance. The limited number of residences ensures an intimate and highly private environment.

02 | Design Philosophy

A seamless architectural promenade.

The building is designed to guide residents through a carefully curated sequence of living spaces framed by light, landscape and panoramic views. Rooted in a contemporary interpretation of the Athenian Riviera lifestyle, the architecture celebrates openness, fluidity and effortless living.

Generous terraces, open-plan interiors and floor-to-ceiling glazing dissolve the boundaries between indoors and outdoors — a continuous dialogue with nature, sunlight and the Mediterranean climate. Warm natural materials, integrated greenery and refined detailing reinforce a sense of understated luxury.

Architectural design — Petropoulou Architects

03 | Location — Glyfada

The heart of the Athenian Riviera.

Fleming Residences enjoy a prime position within one of the most desirable areas of the Athenian Riviera — combining the calm of an upscale residential neighborhood with immediate access to premium amenities.

Just moments from the Marina and waterfront, residents enjoy beaches, yacht clubs, seaside promenades and renowned dining destinations. Glyfada's vibrant center — luxury boutiques, cafés and a dynamic social scene — is within close reach, alongside golf facilities, wellness centers, international schools and curated retail.
